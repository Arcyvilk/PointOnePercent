Ranking

Ranking system utilizes the games' score system. Depending on the game's individual difficulty level, it is given one of three possible marks: ✓, ☆ or ★. ✓ means a relatively tame difficulty, ☆ is already much harder, while ★ means the most possibly hardcore difficulty level.

Completing a game might mean earning its most demanding achievement, or getting the in-game 100%; but for the sake of simplicity the ranking system present here assumes that completing a game means earning 100% of its Steam achievements. Depending on the game's dfficulty level you're being awarded either 1, 2 or 3 points for completing it. Your points are then summarized and used to determine your placemet on the ranking ladder.
